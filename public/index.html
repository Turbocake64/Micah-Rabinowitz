About Me:

Résumé!

A recent Seattle transplant back in the US after 5 years of intercultural communications and project management work in Japan, I am a certified MERN stack web developer who meets every new experience as a learning opportunity.

I see a lot of similarites between web development and my time living in Japan; the key to making progress is being comfortable with feeling uncomfortable and not being afraid of saying "I don't know". Just as with real world language, learning the syntax, flow, and quirks of programming languages has made it possible for me to build new things and connect with new people.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio! Feel free to poke around and shoot me an email!
